Trinity is a strategist and serial entrepreneur specializing in concept, support, and growth development. She utilizes a different methodology of mindset and perspective with an approach based on intention and awareness. She is the co-founder of MSTRAT: Mission Strategy, a strategic production company building concepts from start to finish. Trinity is a voice for this generation, an inspiring writer, author, and speaker for self help, self growth, and self mastery. She is a Vietnamese American icon advocating for mental health, and is currently the founder and CEO for her own health and wellness company, The Still Project.
